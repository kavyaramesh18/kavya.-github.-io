Bengaluru

Bengaluru, also known as Bangalore, is the capital city of the Indian state of Karnataka. It is a major center for information technology and is known as the "Silicon Valley of India". The city has a pleasant climate and is home to many parks, museums, and historical landmarks.

Vidhana Soudha

Legislative building in Bengaluru, India, housing the state legislature of Karnataka. An iconic landmark known for its unique architecture.

Lalbagh

240-acre botanical garden in Bengaluru, India, home to a wide variety of exotic plants and trees, and a popular tourist spot.

Bangalore Palace

Majestic palace located in Bengaluru, India, built in the Tudor-style architecture, with beautiful gardens and ornate interiors.

ISCON Temple

Grand temple in Rajajinagar area of Bengaluru, dedicated to Lord Krishna and Radha, with beautiful architecture and peaceful atmosphere.
